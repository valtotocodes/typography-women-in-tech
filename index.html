Ladies in Tech

"Ladies in Tech in Kenya" is a significant and relevant topic given the ongoing efforts to promote gender equality and empower women in the technology sector. To improve and develop this topic, we can outline several SMART goals to promote and support women in tech in Kenya.

Goals for Improvement

Goal 1

Increase Female Participation in Tech Education

Specific: Launch a scholarship program for 100 female students to pursue technology-related courses in Kenyan universities and technical institutions.

Relevant: Raising awareness will help challenge stereotypes and promote a more inclusive tech industry.

Measurable: Reach a target audience of 500,000 people through various media channels within one year.
